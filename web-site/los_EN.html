The boat

Solent Saracen, K 3649, 1974, designed by Doug Peterson, Contessa 35, Gumboots amd Saracen are productin Contessa 35, with a race deck, considered by Doug Peterson as developments of Ganbare, winner the previous year (1973).
Solent Saracen was built for Mr John McCarthy.

Its history

  • Bought by Mr Bruley des Varannes who named it "Lady Of Solent", F 6450
  • 1975
    • Course de l'EDHEC, Pornic: 7th in IOR
    • 100 milles de La Trinité: 4th Classe I à III
    • La Trinité-La Rochelle: ?
    • Semaine de La Rochelle: 2nd/10 Classe I à III,
  • 1976 Cowes-Dinard : 1st Classe III,
  • 1977 Channel Race : 2nd/22 Classe III, rating : 27.5
Since then, the boat was bought by Mr Christian Devaux, who named it Daulen VI.
Transferred to Passe-Coque and registered with Maritime Affairs on August 6, 2024 under the name "Lady of Solent"..

The project

First a refit, and then classical regatas, with a crew of young people in difficulties.
The project's goal:
Install the Toud Gward anit-collision system to make it available to visually and/or hearing impaired crews.
In partnership with Toud Gward.
