Jolly Jumper, Water Family, Vendée Cœur, Handi Voile

Departing from Les Sables d'Olonnes in this year's Vendée Globe, Jolly-Jumper introduces navigation to several supportive and responsible associations since the start of his renaissance with Water Family. https://waterfamily.org/.
In 2023, discovery days for young autistic people were organized with the Vendée Cœur association.
IN 2024 these operations will be renewed and the Régates Choletaises association (HANDIVOILE FFV club) which allows disabled people to sail in light sails thanks to specific equipment will use Jolly-Jumper to extend its activity towards habitable sailing.

Jolly jumper, end of the cycle "sport santé", towards Bourgenay.
