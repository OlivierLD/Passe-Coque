The boat

Built in Saint Philibert in the famous EZAN shipyard, this gulf flat, ancestor of the Guépard, belonged to Eugène Riguidel who regularly comes to see the evolution of the Passe-Coque shipyard. He became the owner last spring and, with the help of Kevin Pahl, who was Mike Birch's second in command, will be brought back to life to sail in Quiberon Bay with our Listeners. She will soon be anchored in front of the chapel of St Philibert and share the maritime history of our town. We of course need all those who want to help its revival by participating in the shipyard or more simply by donating. Go to www.passe-coque.com or the attached QR code.
