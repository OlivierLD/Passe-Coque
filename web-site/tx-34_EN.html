Project Passages

Hydrophone, the Théâtre de Lorient and Radio Balises are joining forces in a cultural cooperation approach to build the Passages project led with Sauvegarde 56. Over the course of meetings and exchanges held as part of the project, the participants' paths crossed that of Passe-Coque, opening up new perspectives. This project thus aims to encourage public participation in the boat rehabilitation actions led by Passe-Coque, to promote meetings with the teams involved and to offer the opportunity to sail.

Details

In partnership with Sauvegarde 56 and Radio Balises, the "Salle de musiques actuelles" Hydrophone and Le Théâtre de Lorient Centre Dramatique National are coordinating the Passages twinning project, which has been bringing together the disciplines of contemporary music, theatre and radio since 2023.
The twinning project is aimed at homeless people accommodated in LHSS-Lits Halte Soin Santé and ACT-Appartement de coordination thérapeutique of Sauvegarde 56. Some actions will be open to residents in CHRS-Centre d'hébergement et de réinsertion sociale in order to open up to a heterogeneity of the beneficiaries concerned.
Passages takes place over 3 years, the project brings together the disciplines of contemporary music, theatre and radio. Two artists are working on Passages, Lezzi, a musician and sound designer for theatre, and Ojûn, a musician and composer.
With Passages, the beneficiaries explore places of Morbihan's maritime and historical heritage, every place has a story. These are also the places that will be explored through what we call recorded walks, sound walks, through sound, the walk, we will discover, rediscover places, spaces, of its harbor and by going to meet local actors (artists, mediators, experts of the territory, etc.).
Sound postcards in the form of podcasts are produced each year and broadcast on the airwaves of Radio Balises, as well as on the platforms of partner structures.
Culture health project subsidized by the DRAC regional directorate of cultural affairs, financed by Sauvegarde 56, Hydrophone - Salle de Musiques Actuelles and the Théâtre de Lorient - Centre Dramatique National
