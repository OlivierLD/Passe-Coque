A few Arpèges for a Choral navigation!

The Arpège from the Dufour shipyards is a legendary boat in the development of pleasure boating in France.
Designed by Michel Dufour, it was built in more than 1500 copies.
The Arpège was released in 1967. At the Boat Show that year, it was the Arpège that was a great success, which was unexpected because its price was high compared to other boats in its category. This sailboat would quickly become the emblem of the Dufour brand, allowing it to become the leading French and then European manufacturer. It would undergo only a few transformations during the 9 years it was built.
