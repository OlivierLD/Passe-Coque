Unusual light hgabitat

Hublow is a project supported by the Passe‑Coque association in its feasibility phase.
In an ecological and "Low Tech" approach, Hublow aims to offer a new offer of unusual lightweight housing by upcycling old abandoned sailboats.
Hublow thus wishes to provide an innovative, ecological and practical response to housing and attractiveness issues in the Morbihan region.
