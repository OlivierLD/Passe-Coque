La Rêveuse

A strong Damien 40 in steel built at Meta and finished as an amateur by Alain Buisson's father who entrusted it to Passe‑Coque and which Alain and Yann prepare in Saint‑Philibert between two crew training sessions on Ma Enez or Saudade.
  • Year of construction: 1987
  • Length: 12.5 m
  • Draft: 1.10 m / 3.00 m
  • Material: steel
  • Fuel: Diesel
