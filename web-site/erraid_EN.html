Aloes 18, based in Saint Philibert

The ALOES 18 is a fun and accessible sailboat. Its motto: simplicity!

Its large cockpit and flush deck allow for easy and safe maneuvering, while offering ample space for leisure at anchor.

Quick to rig and easy to launch thanks to its liquid ballast, the ALOES 18 can explore any body of water without the constraints of a marina berth. Its fully retractable centerboard allows it to approach close to shore and beach itself on its flat bottom without needing legs.

Stable thanks to its wide hull, the ALOES 18 is also light and responsive under sail, offering exhilarating sensations without requiring significant physical exertion from its passengers. Very forgiving, it will be an excellent companion for learning and improving your sailing skills.

The ALOES 18 combines handcrafted production in France, the lowest possible price, and a maximum reduction in environmental impact thanks to its plywood-epoxy construction.
The majority of the boat (60%) is made of natural materials!

Main features

  • LOA: 5m50
  • Beam: 2m30
  • Draft: 0.10 - 1.30 m
  • Displacement ready to go: 490kg
  • Ballast: 200 kg (160 kg ballast + 40kg center board)
  • Ail area: 20m² (main 12.5m², solent 7.5m²)
  • Material: Plywood + epoxy
