From North to South

The story of a meeting!
Meetings on sailboats are like Russian nesting dolls! Each encounter leads to another! A survey led to Christine and Dominique, which in turn led to Violaine, who... and so on. Following this chain reaction, Shazzan was one of the first sailboats to embark on the joint adventure of Passe-Coque and Nav Solidaires, transporting orthopedic prostheses to Gambia.
Since that first voyage, like La Cardinale and Pen Kaled, Shazzan has become one of our ambassadors, promoting a certain socially responsible and community-minded vision of sailing. They then embarked on a very special Passe-Coque mission with Boris from Nav Solidaires. This adventure will take them to Patagonia to connect the Henri Carette School Group in Roubaix, in northern France, with the school in Puerto Williams, in southern South America, and to discuss the Passe-Coque project and — why not? — continue working on the possibility of a shared Passe-Coque boat there; Bernard and La Cardinale had already initiated this dream.
The Passe-Coque crews are ready!
