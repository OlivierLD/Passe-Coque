JERICHO

  • Designer: Dick Carter
  • Year: 1966
  • LOA: 13m85
  • Rigging: Sloop
  • Beam: 3m70
  • Where: in Marseille Vieux-Port, quai des classiques à la SNM.
  • Draft: 2m10
  • Builder: Frans Maas
  • Material: Steel
  • Displacement: 13 t

Details

Dick Carter, the architect of Pordin Nancq, the Carter 37 of Passe-Coque, did not know that we could still save Jericho when he came to the Carter Cup that we organized in 2023 in La Trinité sur Mer.
Jericho, like Pordin Nancq in 79, participated in the Fastnet 1967 under the name of Gerfault.
Connoisseurs of the Tina will recognize in this boat the characteristic shapes of the Carter. But Jericho needs care and its owners for 30 years are obliged to pass the baton and would like to avoid deconstruction.
Its steel hull must undergo a complete overhaul. Dismantle the interior equipment, sand and treat the hull from the inside, reassemble the equipment and take the opportunity to overhaul the electricity and plumbing as well as the mechanics so that Jericho can live again.
A pool consisting of an integration site and Classics enthusiasts is being set up around Passe-Coque to "revitalize" it and get it sailing again as part of social and solidarity actions.
However, the situation is quite urgent as the boat must leave the SNM before the end of the year. Do not hesitate to ask us about the project via contact@passe-coque.com.
