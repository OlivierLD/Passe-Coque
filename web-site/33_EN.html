Sharing

Sharing, this is Passe‑Coque's DNA. For us, boating is not a goal, this is a mean. A mean to go places, of course, but also to travel, to discover places and people, and to share.
We do intend to extend our boats' lives beyond the projects they were re-born in.

The Passe-Coque Club officially opens its doors for affordable sailing from May 2024.
Our club is open to all our members, and new members, even beginners, and adapted to everyone's means.
Handling support specific to each boat will be put in place by the representatives.

To enjoy the joys of sailing cruising on one of our 6 units available this year, we invite you to send us a pre-registration request by email before April 21, 2024.
PLEASE NOTE: places are limited to only 30 members this year!!

Find our membership conditions and how we operate by clicking HERE.
Find our charter by clicking HERE

To validate your pre-registration, send a request by email to: pcc@passe-coque.com with the following elements:

  • Your motivations for joining the Club (a few lines)
  • Your Nautical Resume (example HERE). The club remains open to beginner and inexperienced members.
  • In the case of a registration request with a reduced price membership, please send us the required documents

A draw will be carried out on Monday April 22, 2024 to select the first 30 members of the Passe-Coque Club. We will communicate the results to you during the day.
The final registration can then be finalized on our website between April 22 and 28, 2024.

The boat reservation schedule will be open as soon as registration is finalized.
For any questions, contact us at the following address: pcc@passe-coque.com
