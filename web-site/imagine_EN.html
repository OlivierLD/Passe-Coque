Selection 37

The boat

The Selection 37 sailboat is a racing-cruising monohull, built by the Jeanneau shipyard. This sailboat, designed by naval architect Michel Joubert, measures 10.87 m long. Production started in 1984 and ended in 1991. 250 examples of the boat were produced. It was designed for the Tour de France under sail (which Jimmy , Pordin Nancq's referent, won 3 times!) Imagine was built in 85. It is under construction in Ouistreham until the end of spring 2024
