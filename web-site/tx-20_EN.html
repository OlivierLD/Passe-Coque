A note about Open Source

Open Source - as per its name - refers to those projects where all the source code to put it to work is available. To put it to work, it might be useful to have some knowledge about IT... You do not give a book (with no image) to someone who cannot read...
The Open Source mantra is "Here is the way I did it, it's all there".
Those who - seeing it - say "Ah it's cool, but you should also add this or that" got the wrong address... If "this" or "that" have to be added, it's the job of the guy who said it, this is called "contribute to the project".
