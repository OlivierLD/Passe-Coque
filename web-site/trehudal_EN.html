The boat

Boat given to Passe‑Coque by Yves Milza (Transquadra 2003, 2004 with Bernard de Ravignan the same year as PJJ).
The Nicholson 33 sailboat is a racing-cruising monohull, built by the Nicholson shipyard.
This fixed keel sailboat, rigged as a Sloop at the head, designed by naval architect Ron Holland, measures 9.77 m in length.
Production started in 1976 and ended in 1979.
